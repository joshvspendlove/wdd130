Manti Temple

While exploring the temple grounds in Manti, Utah, I came across this flower bed at the bottom of the hill. After moving around a bit to find the best angle, this was the photo was taken. This is one of my all time favorite photos, with it's bright colors and beautiful flowers, it majestically balances out with the white temple in the background.
